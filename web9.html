Welcome to Monal Restaurant Islamabad

Monal Restaurant Islamabad offers breathtaking views of the Margalla Hills with delicious traditional and international cuisine. Established in 2006, it is a favorite spot for locals and tourists.

About Monal Restaurant

Located at Pir Sohawa, Monal is known for its fine dining experience combined with stunning views of Islamabad. The restaurant specializes in traditional Pakistani dishes and international flavors.
